About this tool

"A farm emission calculator tool is a tool designed to estimate and track the greenhouse gas emissions produced by agricultural activities on a farm. It helps farmers assess their environmental impact and identify areas where they can implement strategies to reduce emissions. The tool typically takes into account various factors that contribute to emissions, such as livestock populations, fertilizer use, energy consumption, and waste management."
